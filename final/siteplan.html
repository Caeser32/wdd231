Caesar_Siza Animation & Design Studio

Website Project Site Plan

Site Name

Caesar_Siza Animation & Design Studio

This name was chosen because it directly represents my personal brand and creative identity as an animator and designer. "Caesar_Siza" is my established online alias used across platforms including my YouTube channel (@caesar_siza9698), making it instantly recognizable to my existing audience and establishing consistent branding across the web.

Site Purpose

This website serves as a professional portfolio and information hub for my animation and design work under the Caesar_Siza brand. The site will showcase my animation projects, creative work, and embedded videos from my YouTube channel. It provides visitors with a way to explore my portfolio, learn about my background and journey in animation, and reach out for collaboration or commission inquiries through a contact form. The site aims to establish a professional online presence that combines my creative identity with web development skills.

Scenarios

Scenario 1: "I saw one of Caesar_Siza's animations on YouTube and want to see more of their work. Where can I browse through all their animation projects and creative pieces?"

Scenario 2: "I'm interested in commissioning Caesar_Siza for a custom animation project. How do I get in touch and what information do I need to provide?"

Scenario 3: "I'm a fellow animator who wants to learn more about Caesar_Siza's background and journey in animation. Where can I find that information?"

Typography

Two Google Fonts have been selected to balance creativity with readability:

Montserrat (Bold 700, Semi-Bold 600)

Used for: headings (h1, h2, h3), navigation links, and the site logo/name. This geometric sans-serif font conveys a modern, professional feel suited for a creative studio.

Open Sans (Regular 400, Semi-Bold 600)

Used for: body text, paragraphs, form labels, descriptions, and footer content. Open Sans is highly readable at all sizes, making it ideal for longer content and portfolio descriptions.
