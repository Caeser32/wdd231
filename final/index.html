About Me

Hi, I'm Tumisang Dumisani Msiza, the creator behind Caesar_Siza. Based in Pretoria, South Africa, I specialize in character animation, story time videos, and graphic design. My work blends personal storytelling with vibrant character art to create animations that connect with audiences.

From sketch to screen, I use tools like Adobe Animate, After Effects, Procreate, and Illustrator to bring my characters and stories to life. Whether it's a relatable story time episode or a detailed character design sheet, every project is crafted with passion and purpose.
